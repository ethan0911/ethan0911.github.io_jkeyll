Bio

I'm a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at the Scientific Computing and Imaging Institute at the University of Utah, working with Christopher R. Johnson and Ingo Wald. Currently, I am working on the project OSPRay which is supported by the Intel Parallel Computing Centers (IPCC). Before joining the program at Utah, I work as a visiting scholar at School of Geographical Sciences & Urban Planning, Arizona State University. I obtained my Ph.D. in Cartographic and Geographic Information System at the Institute of Remote Sensing and Digital Earth, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interests cover a range of areas in Scientific Visualization, GeoVisualization, High Performance Computing and Spatiotemporal Data Visualization and Mining.
